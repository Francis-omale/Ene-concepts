Ene Concepts-a subsidiary company of SEECOM NIGERIA LIMITED was established in November 2021 for the purpose of:

  • Bead making
  • Packaging of Various food flours-Beans, Cassava,Garri etc

MISSION STATEMENT

  • To be a major player in an industry where excellence is the standard.

  • To take bead making to the next level where culture is an embodiment of creativity.

Bead Making.

Beads has a way of styling women in the africa way.The creativity of the bead has a way of bringing out the africa beauty in women. At Ene-Concept,the beads are carefully selected with quality bead materials and fabric,and are constructed with different colors and sizes which enable different recreation .

Aim And Objective

As clearly stated in our mission statement, the main aim and objective of ene-concept is to bring to fore the art of bead making,where various cultures of the various ethnic tribes of the world could be highlighted.

Other Aims includes:

  • Highlighting the beauty of beads as a better form of jewelry

  • To be an active player in bead making thereby having a fair share of the market.

  • Packaging of various food flours under the most hygienic condition thereby prolonging the life span of the products.

  • Creating employment for the populace by the various activities.
